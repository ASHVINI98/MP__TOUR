CHITRAKOOT




ABOUT

Owing to its mythological significance and natural beauty, Chitrakoot is popularly described as 'the place of many wonders'.

The hindu trinity of Brahma, Vishnu and Mahesh are believed to have taken their incarnation in Chitrakoot.

Chitrakoot is believed to have got its name owing to the presence of 'cheetal' (deer) in the region.According to folklore, Lord Ram and Sita spent eleven of their fourteen years of exile in the deep jungles of Chitrakoot.
