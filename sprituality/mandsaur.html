MANDSAUR




ABOUT

Mandsaur is believed to be the home town of Mandodari, wife of Ravana.

On the bank of river Shivna, sits the grand Pashupatinath Temple, in the holy and historic town of Mandsaur. The spiritual aura of the temple embraces the whole town. What also makes the town special is its treasure of archaeological sites.

Known for its treasure of archaeological sites.

The famous Pashupatinath Temple attracts devotees in huge numbers every year
